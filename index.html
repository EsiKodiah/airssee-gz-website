Project Objectives

Five interconnected objectives driving comprehensive mine reclamation transformation

01

Satellite & Drone Analysis

Acquire and process Sentinel-2 and Landsat-8 imagery alongside high-resolution drone surveys to assess soil conditions, vegetation patterns, and erosion risks across mining regions.

02

AI Model Development

Develop CNN models for land classification, Random Forest algorithms for soil contamination mapping, and NDVI-based regression for vegetation recovery prediction.

03

Field Validation

Conduct ground-truthing at 30-50 mining sites, validating AI predictions through portable test kits, laboratory analysis, and real-world performance assessment.

04

Decision Support System

Build a web dashboard and Progressive Web App integrating IoT sensors, satellite imagery, and predictive analytics for mining stakeholders and regulators.

05

Policy Integration

Organize workshops, develop policy briefs, create AI-based monitoring protocols compatible with regulatory frameworks, and facilitate adoption through pilot projects.

Research Methodology

A comprehensive six-phase approach combining technology, field validation, and stakeholder engagement

01

Data Collection & Preprocessing

Multi-temporal satellite imagery acquisition, high-resolution drone surveys, IoT sensor deployment, and comprehensive environmental sampling across selected mining sites.

Sentinel-2 & Landsat-8 Drone Surveys IoT Monitoring Soil & Water Analysis
02

AI Model Development & Training

Developing U-Net CNNs for land cover classification, ensemble machine learning for contamination mapping, and time-series analysis for vegetation recovery prediction.

CNN Architecture Random Forest XGBoost NDVI Modeling
03

Field Validation & Refinement

Comprehensive ground-truthing campaigns, spectroradiometer measurements, portable XRF validation, and iterative model refinement based on field performance.

30-50 Sites Ground Surveys Lab Analysis Model Optimization
04

System Integration & Deployment

Building scalable web applications with React.js, developing mobile PWAs for field use, creating real-time visualization dashboards, and implementing automated data pipelines.

Web Dashboard Mobile App Cloud Infrastructure API Integration
05

Stakeholder Engagement & Policy

Conducting technical training workshops, organizing community engagement sessions, developing policy briefs and guidelines, establishing regulatory pilot projects.

Workshops Policy Briefs EIA Integration Community Participation
06

Evaluation, Scaling & Dissemination

Publishing research findings, conducting impact assessments, developing scaling strategies for nationwide coverage, and establishing long-term sustainability plans.

Journal Publications Expert Review Scaling Framework Open Data
